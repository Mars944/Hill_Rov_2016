Arm gamepad == Sony PLAYSTATION(R)3 Controller

gamepads are treated as joysticks.

Button Mapping:
- Up D-Pad == Button 4
- Right D-Pad == Button 5
- Down D-Pad == Button 6
- Left D-Pad == Button 7
- Left Bumper == Button 8
- Right Bumper == Button 9
- Left Trigger == Button 10
- Right Trigger == Button 11
